Full Bio

Babatunde Ganiu Adeola is an IT professional with over years of experience in the industry. He currently works as an IT Support Officer at AVON HMO, where he is part of the team that manages IT infrastructure and vendor management to drive continuous growth in the HMO business. Ganiu holds a Higher National Diploma in Computer Science from Moshood Abiola Polytechnic and a Diploma Certificate in Information Technology from National Institute of Information Technology (NIIT). He is currently pursuing a diploma degree in cloud engineering at Altschool Africa.

During his time in higher education, he served as the Student Union President, representing over 25,000 students. He was recognized for his strategic vision and creativity, successfully launching numerous high-profile campaigns that earned him accolades within the student community. His expertise spans system administration, IT support, and cloud computing.

In addition to his professional achievements, Ganiu is passionate about mentoring young secondary school students, engaging with youth, and providing humanitarian services. Outside work, he enjoys discussing current affairs and the state of the nation, meeting new people, and volunteering with local non-profits.
